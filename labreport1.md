## 2. remote connecting

first, open terminal in your VScode
then next, type the command below where zz replaced by the letters in your own account

`$ ssh cs15lwi23zz@ieng6.ucsd.edu`

after that, you will be required to enter your own ucsd cse account password. Enter the password then you should see the screen like this
